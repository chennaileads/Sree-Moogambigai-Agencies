About Company

Trusted Industrial &
Medical Gas Supplier

An ISO 9001: 2015 certified company. Our sole proprietorship firm was established in the year 1995 in Thiruvanmiyur Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. We are the leading and most prominent trader, distributor and Supplier of Industrial, Medical and Special Gas Cylinders. Till date the firm is the first choice of clients for their requirement of industrial gases and other gases.

The sole proprietor of the firm, Mr. Amarnath V has been into business from a long time and has excellent entrepreneur skills and acumen. clients prefer to buy their products from us. We are renowned for always keeping in stock a large products range with all capacities of gas storage Cylinders for the clients to choose from as per their requirements.

The firm is into trading, distributing and supplying Oxygen Gas Cylinders, Nitrogen Gas Cylinders, Carbon Dioxide Gas Cylinders, Dissolved Acetylene Gas, Liquide Oxygen, Hydrogen Gas Cylinders, Helium Gas Cylinder, UHP Argon Gas Cylinders and more.
