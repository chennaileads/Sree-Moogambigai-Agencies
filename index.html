About Company

Leading Industrial Gas
Supplier Since 1995

Sree Moogambigai Agencies is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company established in the year 1995 at Thiruvanmiyur, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. We are one of the leading traders, distributors and suppliers of Industrial, Medical and Special Gas Cylinders for various industries.

Trusted Quality & Reliable Supply

With years of industry experience and strong business expertise under the leadership of Mr. Amarnath V, our company has become the preferred choice for industrial gas requirements. We maintain a wide stock of gas cylinders in different capacities to meet customer needs with timely delivery and dependable service.
  • Supplier of Oxygen, Nitrogen, Carbon Dioxide, Hydrogen, Helium and Special Gases.
  • Large inventory of Industrial, Medical and High Purity Gas Cylinders with multiple capacities.
  • Reliable delivery, quality assurance and customer-focused service support.
